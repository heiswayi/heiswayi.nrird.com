Last year, I have a chance to visit Loveland, Colorado, USA in January 2018. It's a beautiful place. I have been there for 2 weeks, sent by my company to work on product knowledge transfer with my colleagues in Keysight Loveland.

And at the same time, I have a chance to visit some beautiful places around in Colorado such as Rocky Mountain National Park, Estes Park, and some other places.

I wish I have a chance to visit Loveland again in the future.

Just another dedicated site for my Loveland, Colorado, USA trip photography: https://labs.nrird.com/loveland
